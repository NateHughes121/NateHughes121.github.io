Travel to Japan!

Tokyo

The 23 special wards of Tokyo, although each is a city, constitute the area informally known as the "City of Tokyo."

  • View the cherry blossoms in Shinjuku Gyoen
  • Go shopping in the Akihabara district
  • Climb the Tokyo Skytree and see Mount Fuji

Kyoto

Once the capital of Japan, Kyoto is famous for its beautiful temples and dense forests.

  • See the Kinkaku-ji Temple
  • Walk through the dense bamboo forest in Arashiyama
  • Try authentic sushi in a local restaurant
